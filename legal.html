Cancellation Policy

Nomads Hideaway maintains a split cancellation policy designed to protect both the guest's flexibility and the hosts' interests.

Cancellation Timeline & Refund Rates

  • More than 60 days before arrival: 100% refund (minus 10% non-refundable processing fee)
  • 30-59 days before arrival: 50% refund
  • 14-29 days before arrival: 25% refund
  • Less than 14 days before arrival: No refund (100% retained)
  • No-show or cancellation on arrival date: 100% charge with no refund
Non-Refundable Fee: A 10% administrative and credit card processing fee is non-refundable in all cases to cover costs incurred at the time of booking.

Special Circumstances

While we enforce a strict policy, we strongly recommend all guests purchase comprehensive **Travel Insurance**. We do not offer refunds for "Force Majeure" events (weather, flight cancellations, global health issues) beyond the rates stated above.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Cleaning fees (non-refundable in all cases)
  • Service fees charged by booking platforms
  • Deposits and security fees (unless damages are not incurred)
