Cancellation Policy

Nomads Hideaway maintains a firm cancellation policy designed to protect the hosts' interests and ensure business stability.

Cancellation Timeline & Refund Rates

  • More than 60 days before arrival: 50% refund (50% retained by host)
  • 30-60 days before arrival: 25% refund (75% retained by host)
  • 15-29 days before arrival: No refund (100% retained by host)
  • Less than 15 days before arrival: No refund (100% retained by host)
  • No-show or cancellation on arrival date: 100% charge with no refund
Host Protection: These strict terms protect Lionel and Priyanka from last-minute cancellations and ensure consistent income for the property. Last-minute cancellations severely impact the hosts' ability to secure alternative bookings.

Special Circumstances

The hosts may consider partial refunds or rebooking options for genuine emergencies (medical issues, death in family, travel restrictions) at their sole discretion. Requests must be submitted with supporting documentation within 48 hours of cancellation.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Cleaning fees (non-refundable in all cases)
  • Service fees charged by booking platforms
  • Deposits and security fees (unless damages are not incurred)
